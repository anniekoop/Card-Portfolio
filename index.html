About Me

I'm Annie, a product and UX designer based in Salt Lake City. I focus on creating solutions that think ahead and experiences that offer both accessibility and enjoyment. I'm currently building a cross-functional skillset to bridge design with development, fueled by my love for the process of digital product.

In 2021, I moved to London to study journalism at Roehampton University. As an international student, I contributed articles to female-led lifestyle publications across Great Britain. I gained valuable experience during my years there, but ultimately decided that journalism was not the career path for me.

After exploring more technical fields, I came across the Google UX Design program, which challenged me in ways that I had been looking for in previous fields. I spent six months learning from the 7 UX courses, and after earning my certificate, I picked up other skillsets that improved my work.

Currently, I do most of my work in Figma, Visual Studio Code, Webflow, and Creative Cloud.
